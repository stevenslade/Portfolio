Project Image

Hiking Hub

Hiking Hub allows you to enter a city of your choice, and then returns a selection of hiking trails within 15 miles. You are then able to select a trail and the app returns a selection of reddit posts regarding the chosen trail. Hiking Hub will also save your recent searches allowing for a quick search of your favorite locations.

Skills demonstrated:

Google Places API, Reddit API, Tailwind CSS, Materialize CSS, Local Storage
